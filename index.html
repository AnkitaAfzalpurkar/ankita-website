OVERVIEW RECOMMERCE

Recommerce, is a brand of Urdhva Management and the pioneering force in leading the charge towards a brighter future for the industries, A category-defining company dedicated to building a sustainable reverse commerce ecosystem.
 
At Recommerce, we embody the ideals of Engage, Educate, Exchange, Empower, and Enable, propelling industries and individuals towards a brighter future. Our tagline, “Unlocking Infinite Possibilities, Connecting Industries: Pioneering Sustainability for a Thriving Tomorrow!” encapsulates our mission to revolutionize India’s business landscape through Recommerce, our pioneering brand dedicated to driving a circular economy. 

ABOUT THE EVENT

Over the years, we ’ve witnessed several transformative revolutions. The 1900s brought the Automotive Revolution, followed by the Innovation Revolution like TV, Phones, etc. in the late century, and the rise of the Internet in the early 2000s. Today, AI is taking center stage. However, one constant across all these revolutions is the role of electronics, turning what was once unimaginable into reality.  

ITAD | REFURBISH | RECYCLE | REPAIR | REMANUFACTURE

Electronics powered every revolution—now, the Circular Revolution is here to reshape the future!”  

The Global Leaders Meet to discuss WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) and IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) is a premier global event dedicated to advancing circular economy strategies, sustainable business practices, and responsible resource management. This event brings together industry leaders, policymakers, sustainability advocates, technology innovators, manufacturers, recyclers, enablers, and refurbishers from across the globe to explore cutting-edge solutions and drive impactful discussions on the future of electronic waste. 

“WEEE” Rethink!, “WEEE” React!, “WEE” Redesign, “WEEE” Restore!

The event serves as a vital platform for business collaboration, knowledge-sharing, policy discussions, and technological advancements aimed at accelerating the transition toward a sustainable, resource-efficient economy .
